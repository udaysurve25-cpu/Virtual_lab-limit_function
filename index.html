How to Use & Function Syntax

Function Syntax

  • Basic operations: +, -, *, /, ^
  • Trigonometric: sin(x), cos(x), tan(x)
  • Exponential: exp(x) or e^x
  • Logarithmic: log(x) for natural log
  • Square root: sqrt(x)
  • Absolute value: abs(x)
  • Constants: pi, e

Limit Examples

  • sin(x)/x at x→0 = 1
  • (x^2-1)/(x-1) at x→1 = 2
  • (1+1/x)^x at x→∞ = e
  • |x|/x at x→0 = undefined
  • 1/x at x→0 = ∞
